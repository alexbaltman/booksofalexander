Top 5 Questions

Have my books arrived yet?

The US Post Office takes 2-3 weeks. FedEx and UPS have a delivery time of usually less than one week. Please choose the FedEx or UPS shipping method if your shipment contains a minimum of $35 worth of books, to receive free tracking, free insurance, and a faster delivery.
We will send out a 'received' email notification as soon as your shipment is received.

Where’s the money?

After your books arrive and are inspected, payment is made within 3 business days.

Do you buy collectibles or antiques?
We do not buy collectibles or antiques. A good place to sell your book is to someone in the Antiquarian Booksellers' Association of America (ABAA). To obtain its free directory of 461 booksellers, call (212) 944-8291 or visit online at www.abaa.org.

Why are you not buying my books?

We buy a wide variety of a lot of different books; however, most of the books we purchase have a publication date of 2009 or newer. What we buy can change day by day or week by week depending on resale values, our current inventory, demand, and other factors.

How will I ship my books to booksofalexander.com?

Shipping is FREE with our service. Your order should have a mailing label paid for by us that you print right from your computer. If it does not email us and we will resolve any issues.

If you do not have a printer, simply send us an email with "Need Labels" as the subject preferably after you have submitted your books to sell, so that we know which labels to mail you: USPS, FedEx, or UPS labels.

What types of books do you buy?

What types of books do you buy?

We buy a wide variety of books. However, most of the books we purchase have a publication date of 2009 or newer.

What types of books do you not buy?


  • Missing or loose pages.
  • Major wear to the binding.
  • Tears to the cover or pages.
  • Water, moisture damage, mold, or stains.
  • Fire or smoke damage.
  • Strong odor of any kind (including cigarette odor).
  • Exceeds our maximum quantity of each ISBN.
  • Custom edition textbooks (textbooks printed for a specific school).
  • Items with no ISBN printed anywhere on or inside the item.
  • Books that state "review copy", "free copy", "not for resale" (or "not for sale") anywhere on the cover or inside.
  • Teacher's or Instructor's editions.

Do you buy audio books?

Yes, we do buy books on CD (just enter the ISBN like any other paperback or hard cover). However, we do not purchase books on cassette tape.

What is an International Edition?

These are editions of textbooks which are printed and intended for sale in a non-US market. These books may have a different ISBN but usually have the same content as the US version. They are often bound with a soft cover and printed using lower quality materials. They usually have a statement such as "international edition" or "economy edition" and "Not for sale in the USA" somewhere on the cover. Booksofalexander.com does not buy International editions.

Do you buy collectible books or offer appraisals?

We do not buy collectibles or antiques. A good place to sell your book is to someone in the Antiquarian Booksellers' Association of America (ABAA). To obtain its free directory of 461 booksellers, call (212) 944-8291 or visit online at www.abaa.org.

Do you buy books that don’t have an ISBN?

No, we only purchase books that have an ISBN, and usually books that have been published within the last 5 years.

Will you accept books in any condition?

Books will not be accepted with any of the following:
  • Fire or smoke damage
  • Major wear to the binding
  • Medium to large tears to the cover or pages
  • Missing pages
  • Strong odor of any kind (including cigarette odor)
  • Water, moisture damage, or mold
  • Workbooks or manuals with writing in them

Will you purchase books with missing CDs, missing dust jackets, writing, underlining, and/or highlighting?

If a book originally came with a dust jacket, CD or DVD, it must accompany the book when shipped to us or the quoted price may be reduced.
Other conditions that may result in a reduction of the quoted price:
  • Excessive writing, underlining, and/or highlighting
  • Remainder marks on page edges (publisher's overstock)
A small amount of writing, underlining or highlighting of pages is usually okay, as long as it is on fewer than 20% of the book's pages. However, workbooks, lab manuals, study guides, and books with practice tests will not be accepted if they contain writing. If a textbook originally came with a CD or DVD, the original CD/DVD must accompany the book when shipped to us. The CD/DVD must be in working condition. Computer manuals and language courses originally issued with a CD will not be accepted without.
Booksofalexandr reserves the right to determine item condition upon receipt in our warehouse. Items not accepted by Booksofalexander will only be shipped back to the Seller at the Seller's expense. Items not accepted will be held for 30 days; after that time, these items will be recycled or donated at our discretion.

Do you buy ex-library books?

No.

ISBNs

What is an ISBN number?

An ISBN is a 10 or 13 digit number that can be found on the back cover or inside the front cover of almost all books. You may enter the ISBN with or without the dashes, and don't leave off the leading zeros. Sometimes an ISBN can end with an X. Booksofalexander.com will accept either the 10-digit or the 13-digit ISBN. Examples:
978-1-888799-97-2
1-888799-97-8
13-digit ISBNs should start with the numbers 978. Your book may have a 10-digit ISBN, or a 13-digit ISBN, or both (if your book has both, only enter the 13-digit number).

Where do I find a book's ISBN number?

The ISBN is usually found on the back cover, immediately above the barcode.
If you cannot locate your book's ISBN on the outside of the book, open the front cover and look on the page immediately following the title page. This should be the copyright page and will include information about the copyright date, the author and publisher. The ISBN is a 10 or 13 digit number, and it should be in the middle or at the bottom of that page. If the book is pre-1970s, then it may not have an ISBN.

What if my book does not have an ISBN?

Sorry, but we do not purchase older books and/or collectibles without ISBN numbers.

My book has more than one ISBN. Which one should I use?

Please use the ISBN on the copyright page. If there are multiple ISBNs present on the copyright page, select the one that best describes the book. For example, if you are selling a paperback book, choose the ISBN marked as "pbk." or "paperback." Likewise, only select the "package" ISBN if your book includes all original, supplemental materials (e.g. CD-ROMs, unused online access codes, workbooks). If these items are not included, please use the "text only" ISBN.
Often times, bookstores may place their own sticker or unique ISBN on the back cover of a book. For this reason, always use the printed ISBN located on the copyright page.
Please be aware, if the ISBN on the order does not match the ISBN on the copyright page, the book may be removed or the price quote adjusted accordingly.
If you have any questions regarding ISBNs, please feel free to contact us.

How do you decide which books to buy?

We base our prospective purchases on supply and demand in the market. Our database is constantly changing.
How do you determine prices for books?
We constantly evaluate the market's supply and demand. This allows us to offer the best prices. We constantly update our database to insure you get the best prices possible for your books.
We keep our pricing simple and offer one quote for each book that meets, or exceeds, our Used Book Condition Policy. The price quotes are based on the assumption that your books range from very good to new condition, and that they meet all of our Used Book Condition Policy. We reserve the right to determine condition and may take deductions from the original price quote, if necessary.

How does the shipping work?

I want to send the books to you. What do I need to do?

Once you submit your books to us, you should receive an email and instructions. Another way is to login to your Account, and print your label from there.

How long do I have to send the books in?

We ask that you ship your books to us as soon as possible. If you need to ship them more than 7 days after you completed the Checkout process, please contact us.

Where can I get a box?

There are several options. You can buy a new box from USPS or a supply store, or get a used box for free. (Don't forget that if it's a used box, it should still be sturdy and free from any address labels or residue that could damage your books!) Great sources for used boxes:
  1. Your local shopping mall - make sure to ask permission from the manager or security office
  2. The supermarket - ask which days they receive deliveries and catch the boxes before they are compacted for recycling
  3. Ask at individual retail stores, small businesses, doctor's offices - they are often more than willing to get rid of them
  4. Check free ads in your local craigslist.org or Recycler.com

Do I have to pay for shipping to get my books to you?

No. We will pay the shipping and provide instructions after you submit your books.

What if I can’t print?

If you are unable to print, then you have two options:
  1. We can mail you some shipping labels--just request them from us by visiting our contact us page and sending an email.
  2. Pay for shipping initially, then once we receive your books, we will issue you a shipping credit. Inside your package on a sheet of paper, write your order ID number. On the same piece of paper write "Please add a shipping credit." Once your box of books arrive, we will add a shipping credit to your order total. This amount will be the equivalent of USPS Media Mail rate, based on the weight of the books. If you do this, you may wish to ask for Media Mail at your Post Office.

How do I know what’s going on with my books?

We will send out a "received" email notification as soon as your shipment is received. Media Mail takes up to 14 business days whereas FedEx and UPS are much faster. Allow a few days for processing as well. Once your books arrive at our facility they will be inspected. You may also login to view the status of your shipments at any time by going to your account. After your books are inspected and marked as "processed", payment will be issued. Payment is initiated within 1-3 business days of "processed" status.

What if I forget to include the packing slip?

If you let us know, we will look up your shipment by your name or email when it arrives. As long as you put a return address on the package we should have no problems processing your books.

What if I do not include some of the books on the packing slip?

We will send out a "received" email notification and inform you about the missing titles and whether there was any damage to your package. We will hold your items and wait for your reply and after 14 days we will go ahead and process your book(s) as is.

What if I pack the wrong books or a different edition?

We will first check to see whether the items belong to another of your orders. If not and if we are buying the item, we will remove the previous item from your order and add the new edition or book. If we are not buying the items, we will hold them for 30 days; during this time you can pay for return shipping and this time we will recycle or donate the books.

What if I include books in the box that are not on the packing slip?

We ask sellers not to include books that are not on the packing slip. We will first check to see whether the items belong to another of your orders. If not and if we are buying the item, we will remove the previous item from your order and add the new edition or book. If we are not buying the items, we will hold them for 30 days; during this time you can pay for return shipping and after this time we will recycle or donate the books.

Can I use a different shipping carrier?

You may choose to use a different shipping carrier, we will only credit you the USPS Media Mail rates, based on the weight of the books. The books will be weighed on arrival.

Payment

How long until I get paid?

If you mail your books from a USPS Office using our pre-paid label, it can take 7-14 days for the books to arrive here. We will send out a "received" email notification as soon as your shipment is received. Once your books are received, they will be inspected. After your books are inspected and marked as "processed", payment will be issued. We will mail you a check, or pay you via PayPal, within 3 business days after we receive and process your book(s).

How do I get paid?

We will mail you a check, or pay you via PayPal, or if you are in an approved zip code you can get cash.

How does PayPal work?

PayPal allows you to receive money electronically and then transfer the money to a regular bank account (or you may keep the money in your PayPal account). All we need to send you money via PayPal is your email address. If you do not yet have a PayPal account, PayPal will send you instructions on how to set one up.

When registering for an account with Booksofalexander.com, you are given the choice of a PayPal or check payment preference or cash. We encourage you to choose PayPal, as it is fast, convenient, and more secure than a check. Existing users may also choose to later change their payment preference to PayPal, by changing their preference in their account page.

From PayPal's website: "With more than 40 million member accounts in over 45 countries worldwide, PayPal is the world's largest online payment service. PayPal makes sending money as easy as sending email! Any PayPal member can instantly and securely send money to anyone in the U.S. with an email address."

Why doesn't the amount that I was paid match my original Booksofalexander quote?

Once books are received, they are inspected. If we receive a book that does not meet our Condition Policy, the quote price for that book may be deducted from your total payment. Books that we are unable to purchase are kept for 30 days, during which time they may be returned at the Seller's expense.


Why don't you offer a premium for "New" or "Like New" books?

The two main reasons are time and efficiency. We have received a great deal of positive feedback for the ease of use of our site, and it has been designed for the purchase of buying used books. If you are a new book wholesaler or distributor and would like to establish a business relationship with us, please contact us.


I've lost or accidentally thrown away my check! What do I do?

The best thing to do is to send us an email using the contact us form. Make sure you state that your check has been lost or misplaced and that you will need a new one. Be sure to also include the following:


  • your name
  • mailing address
  • Order ID #
  • amount of the check (if you know it)

In most cases we are able to re-issue lost checks within a reasonable period of time.
